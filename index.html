All About Me!

My name is Ashley Duffy and I am Junior at Bellbrook High School.

I was born in Kettering, Ohio. I lived in Dayton Ohio for a few years an then moved to Athens, Ohio. After moving around a few more times, my family settled in Bellbrook Ohio in 2006 and I have been there ever since.
